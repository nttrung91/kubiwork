Pressa

Pressa is a simple and easy to use web app that enables autistic children express themselves. Using a collection of common words, minimalistic icons, and a text to speech API, Pressa brings out an autistic child’s Inner Voice. Simply press on a combination of word tiles to form a phrase, then press the Speaker Icon for Pressa to read the phrase aloud. This app was created in less than two weeks for the 2014 Autism Appjam competition at UCI.

Strategy

Pressa is a web application that helps autistic kids to communicate with other people around them. The app was built by a group of five UC Irvine students. It was built within 7 days during an Autism Appjam Competition.

During the built process, I was in charge of planning, building, and providing direction to what to do next.
